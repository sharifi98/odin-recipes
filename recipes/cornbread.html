Cornbread

These muffins are very similar to the ones found in a famous down-home cooking restaurant.

They're so simple to make and taste great because they have real pieces of corn as well as corn meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, sugar and honey
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes
